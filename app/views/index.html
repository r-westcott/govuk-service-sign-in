Sign in to a government service

{{releaseVersion | log }}

Different government services have different ways to sign in - Government Gateway, Defra account, Verify, and so on. The purpose of this page is to make it easier to find government accounts

Initial page

Initially we propose publishing a page on GOV.UK that:

  • signposts users to the most commonly used services on GOV.UK
  • lets people search for other services not on the list (either on the page or by funnelling them to the GOV.UK search page)
  • does not talk about the GOV.UK account yet
Initial version (no GOV.UK account)

How the page could evolve over time

There will be an awkward phase of some services being accessible through the account and some being accessed in other ways. This version includes the GOV.UK account as an option and outlines the services that can be accessed with it.

Version with GOV.UK account

Once the GOV.UK account has many more services onboarded, the messaging will need to explain how signing into those services has changed.
